Responsible Gambling Policy

At Online BlackJack Canada, we are committed to promoting responsible gambling and providing a safe and secure environment for all players. Gambling should always be a form of entertainment — never a way to solve financial problems. This policy outlines our efforts to support responsible play and help those who may be at risk.

Why Responsible Gaming Is Important

Responsible gambling is essential for protecting players from the potential harms associated with excessive or uncontrolled play. While most users enjoy online blackjack as a recreational activity, a small percentage may experience negative consequences. Our goal is to help every player stay in control and make informed choices.

Signs of Gambling Problems

If you or someone you know is experiencing any of the following, it may be a sign of a gambling issue:

  • Spending more money or time on gambling than intended

  • Chasing losses or gambling to recover past losses

  • Feeling anxious, depressed, or irritable when not gambling

  • Neglecting responsibilities at work, school, or home

  • Hiding gambling activity from others

  • Borrowing money or selling possessions to fund play

Recognizing these signs early can help prevent more serious problems.

Safe Gambling Recommendations

To maintain a healthy relationship with gambling, follow these tips:

  • Set a budget before playing and never exceed it

  • Limit your playing time and take regular breaks

  • Never gamble when upset, stressed, or under the influence

  • Treat gambling as entertainment — not a way to earn income

  • Avoid chasing losses — there’s no guarantee of winning back money

  • Monitor your activity regularly

Staying mindful of your habits can reduce the risk of harm.

Self-Exclusion and Limits

If you feel your gambling is becoming problematic, you can take action by:

  • Setting deposit limits to control how much you spend

  • Setting time limits to restrict daily or weekly sessions

  • Using self-exclusion tools to take a temporary or permanent break

We encourage all users to use these tools proactively if they feel at risk. These features help promote accountability and provide the space to regain control.

Aid Organizations and Support

If you need help, you are not alone. Several Canadian and international support services offer confidential assistance, including:

  • Gambling counseling hotlines

  • Online therapy programs

  • Peer support groups

  • Professional treatment services

We urge anyone facing gambling-related challenges to reach out for help.

Protection of Minors

We are committed to preventing underage gambling. Our site is strictly intended for individuals aged 18 years or older. To support this:

  • Age verification procedures are enforced

  • Parental control tools are recommended

  • Accounts for minors are strictly prohibited

We urge parents and guardians to monitor their children’s online activities and use software that restricts access to gambling content.
